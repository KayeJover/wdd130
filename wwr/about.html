White Water Rafting

The purpose of our White Water Rafting is to give participants a sense of adventure and is one of the best ways to make memories with their family and friends. It is also a great way to use a team-building activity to challenge themselves on how to work together; it will help them to use good communication skills, cooperation, and having faith among their teammates. Our mission is to provide our participants with unforgettable whitewater rafting experiences that help their physical and mental health through the activities and to connect with nature. Our motto is 'Paddle your way to adventure! '

HIISTORY

White Water Rafting was founded in 1990 by Gilda and Rodolfo Gualingco. The married couple really loves to adventure and explore outdoor activities. One day, they tried to go water rafting. They started with just the two of them to explore using the inflatable rafting boat and paddles.It was challenging for them, but with their teamwork, they made it. The couple wanted to share with others the experience they had,particularly in whitewater rafting. The couple started to build their own company with their children, and with the use of social media, many people know about their rafting activities.
